Introducing James Mullins

James Mullins

Business Owner | Managing Director | Sales Agent | Board Member of First National

James Mullins is the Managing Director of First National Progressive, a real estate agency based in Christchurch, New Zealand. With a sharp focus on strategic sales, marketing, and leadership, he has guided the company to significant regional success and garnered industry recognition for excellence in business development and team performance.

Introducing Lynette Mullins

Lynette Mullins

Business Owner | Managing Director | Sales Agent

Lynette Li is a warm, detail-driven real estate professional who truly puts people first. Fluent in Mandarin and English, with a strong grasp of Cantonese, she builds trust quickly and connects with clients in a personal, culturally attuned way. Her bubbly personality and family-first values make every interaction feel genuine, supported, and stress-free.
